The Plan

Site Name

The site name is going to be: The Diver (the-diver)

The Purpose

The purpose of the main page is to be a tribute to my friend focusing on one of his hobbies that he got an opportunity to do as a side job for fun. This hobby is scuba diving, I will then have links to information about diving certifications (classes and prices) and another page with points of interests that are local around arizona.

Scenarios

Who is the man that dives in the scheels tank in Chandler Arizona? Answers and gives some interesting facts about my friend who is the man in the tank!

Where can you get your scuba certifications? This will give a responsive click to the other page where information will be available on classes and the price of said classes.

Will I be able to use it here in Arizona? This will be another page pointing out points of interests and where they are located within the state.

Colors

The colors I am choosing is a ocean inspired color scheme the colors are: --lapis-lazuli: #05668dff; --coral-pink: #eb9486ff; --cream: #f0f3bdff; --teal: #028090ff; --persian-green: #00a896ff;

Typography

The main two fonts I will be using are volkhov and barlow. Volkhov will be used on headings and barlow will be the main tex of the paragraphs.
